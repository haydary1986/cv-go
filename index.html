Features

Everything you need to build professional CVs with AI-powered assistance

📝

CV Creation

Comprehensive forms covering personal data, experience, education, skills, languages, and academic links with auto-save support.

🎨

16 Templates

Professional templates including Academic, ATS, Creative, Designer, Medical, Minimalist, Modern, Tech, and more.

🤖

AI Integration

3 AI providers (OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek) for text improvement, ATS scoring, cover letters, and job suggestions.

🌐

Bilingual Support

Full Arabic (RTL) and English (LTR) support with dynamic switching and custom fonts (Cairo & Inter).

📄

PDF Export

High-quality PDF export with automatic multi-page support and custom filenames.

🔗

CV Sharing

Unique share links with auto-generated QR codes and view count tracking.
